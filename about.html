About Us

Cyber Congress

Cyber Congress is an annual flagship event of Cyber Security club of Geethanjali College of Engineering and Technology. Cyber Congress 2022 aims at promoting and spreading Cyber awareness in association with End Now Foundation. It aspires to introduce the fundamentals of Cybersecurity and promote awareness on the occasion of Cyber Awareness month themed “It's easy to stay safe online”.

Cyber Congress also provides a platform for students to compete, where they can augment their skills and refine their knowledge in the key aspects of this domain. This year the Congress runs with the theme “It's easy to stay safe online” , promoting formal/non-formal sessions and competitions in the area of Cyber Security

Where

Seminar Hall, GCET

When

Oct. 21. 2022 - Oct. 22. 2022
