About Us

Cyber Congress

Cyber Congress 2022 aims at promoting and spreading mass awareness on various concepts under Cybersecurity. It aspires to introduce the fundamentals of Cybersecurity and promote awareness on the occasion of Cyber Awareness month. Its purpose is to provide high-profile, interactive and leading-edge events for students to exchange ideas, opinions, collaborate and learn from the learned ones.

Cyber Congress also provides a platform for students to compete, where they can augment their skills and refine their knowledge in the key aspects of this domain. Broadly, the Congress with the theme “Cyber Safety and Cyber Security”, envisions promoting formal/non-formal sessions and competitions in the area of Cyber Security aimed at spreading mass awareness on Information/Cyber Security in the institution.

Where

Seminar Hall, GCET

When

Oct. 21. 2022 - Oct. 22. 2022

Cyberlympics

Cyberlympics is a fun and knowledge driven competition series under cyber congress 2022, which are in-person and virtual events, where individuals are allowed to participate in a variety of cyber security challenges and activities. These are competitions which test competitive skills, artistic talents, knowledge levels of participants in the field of cyber security. In the larger canvas of things, they can help individuals recognize the hazards in cyber world and become aware of them. Participation certificates will be given to all the students. Exciting goodies will be given to the Winner and Runner-ups.
